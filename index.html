Humanist Community of Ventura County

The Humanist Community of Ventura County is a community of non-religious people centered in Ventura County, CA.

You can find us on Facebook and Meetup

About

The Humanist Community of Ventura County is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit social group committed to secular values and community service.

We are an association of non-religious individuals and families in and around Ventura County. The HCoVC is an official chapter of the American Humanist Association and the Foundation Beyond Belief.
